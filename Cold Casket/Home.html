Motto and Mission

In Cold Casket, we believe that every one deserves a honourable send-off. Funerals are a time to celebrate the life of our loved one, to reminisce the memories and achievements of their lifetime. Each funeral we set up is carefully curated to combine modern aesthetics with the tradition of religion. Leave No Dead Behind - It is to tell our customers that there is no such thing as a impossible request and that we will cater to all regardless of race, religious and social class a proper rite and funeral as they, or their loved ones, depart for the afterlife.

What We Do
