First off, compose and calm down. Proceed to contact us at 6553 8991 for immediate assistance and advice.

Please obtain a Certificate of cause of death from your doctor if the death occurs in a hospital.

If the location where death occured is at home, please call the family doctor or a neighbourhood doctor who is willing to make a house call to verify the death and issuing of CCOD.

Once a CCOD is issued, our staff from Cold Casket will proceed to shift the decreased body to our office for cleaning, embalming and dressing.

Please do prepare a set of the deceased's clean clothes and hand over to our staff.

Our dedicated funeral planner will then discuss the funeral arrangment with your family.

Do apply for a registration of death at the Registry of Births and Deaths counter in any hospital or any police post.

Do remember to bring the CCOD and ICs of both the deceased and informat and inform the officer of the place for cremation or burial and he/she will issue death certificate and permit for cremation or burial.

Do go to your local town council for void deck usage permits.

Ensure that you made the necessary preparations to receive the decreased and the casket when we send them to the venue of the wake.


Coroner's case

In times where there is no doctors to certify the death, the police will help to arrange for the body to be sent to the Mortuary@HSA, located at Blk 9 of Singapore General Hospital.

The investigative officer from the police will then arrange for the family to view and identify the decreased's body with the presence of the coroner.

The case will be reviewed by the coroner to determine if an autospy is required(meaing examination of body for the cause of death).

The deceased's family will then be informed of the coroner's decision and the date and time to collect the body for the funeral
